Feijoada

Ingredients

  • Black beans
  • Bay leaf
  • Smoked sausage (i.e. kielbasa)
  • Dried sausage (i.e. linguica or Chinese sausage)
  • Bacon (ends/chunks, not strips)

Directions

  1. Chop up all meat into 1 inch chunks
  2. Sear all meat in bottom of big pan
  3. Deglaze bottom of pan with 1 can black beans
  4. Mash 1 can black beans (easier to do in can)
  5. Add both cans of beans and meat into big pot
  6. Add bay leaf
  7. Add as many beans as you want, dilute with water if needed
  8. Simmer for as long as possible, stirring occasionally. Careful not to let bottom burn.
  9. Serve with white rice and spinach / collared greens

Hungarian Burnt Garlic Soup

Serves 8
Source Recipe

Ingredients

  • Parmesan Cheese
  • 6x Garlic Heads
  • 2x onions (diced)
  • 1.4 L stock (chicken or veg)
  • 400 mL heavy cream (sub whole milk or half and half
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Cut tops off garlic heads. Place on baking sheet with foil, drizzle with olive oil, and wrap foil over the tops (wrap individually)
  3. Roast for about 30 minutes
  4. Heat dutch oven on stove with 2 tbsp olive oil
  5. Cook onion ~20 minutes until browned.
  6. Let garlic cool for about 5 minutes then squeeze into pot with onion
  7. Add stock and bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10 minutes
  8. Blend with immersion blender
  9. When ready to serve, add heavy cream, half and half, or whole milk. Garnish with croutons and parmesan cheese or freshly minced garlic on top

Moqueca (Brazilian Fish Stew)

Serves 4-6
Source Recipe 1
Source Recipe 2

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs white fish (cod or halibut), patted dry and cut into pieces
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 bell peppers of various colors (red, orange), diced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp dende oil
  • 2 tbsp grapeseed oil
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2-3 limes, 1 juiced and zested, the rest in wedges to serve
  • 1 cup seafood stock

Initial Cook

  1. Season fish with salt (set aside until Final Cook step)
  2. Heat 2 tbsp oil and 2 tbsp dende oil in large pot until shimmering
  3. Add garlic and onion until onion is translucent
  4. Add bell pepper, paprika, and cayenne pepper
  5. Cook until peppers are softened

Prepare Stew (same saucepan as initial cook)

  1. Add canned tomatoes and cook for 3 minutes
  2. Add juice and zest of 1 lime, 1 tsp salt, 1 can coconut milk, and 1 cup seafood stock
  3. Stir until combined and bring to a boil
  4. Reduce heat and let simmer for 5 minutes

Final Cook

  1. Season fish with salt
  2. Add fish and press down gently to submerge in broth
  3. Cover and return to gentle simmer and cook until fish just cooked through, about 5 minutes
  4. Add more salt or lime as needed
  5. Serve with steamed rice or crusty bread
